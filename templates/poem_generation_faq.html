• Will I get the same poem again if I put in the same word again?

    No. At least, it's very, very, very unlikely. Each time you refresh the poem page or submit a word, the inspiration-generator is at work anew.

  • Where does the data come from??

    The Gutenberg Poetry Corpus, put together by Allison Parrish. Check out her other work on machine-generated poetry and interactive text!

  • Why doesn't my poem have the word I put in as part of its text / rhyme with the word I put in anywhere / seem to have anything to do with the word I put in?

    The word you put in to this site simply inspires the generator — no promises as to how that shows up in the result. Inspiration is complicated.

  • Why does it take so long?

    Like it says, art takes time. If you know me personally, feel free to ask me for more of a story. It's not very interesting, but it is pretty silly.

  • Why isn't there {insert thing you think the site should have} ?

    Either because I didn't make that happen yet, I didn't think of it, or because I'm not going to. :) If you know me personally feel free to make a suggestion, though! I'll probably continue adding features and modifications as long as it's still fun.
